HOW TO PURCHASE Safestarlink

  1. Download MetaMask or Trust Wallet Applications
  2. Purchase Binance Tokens (BNB) and send them to your MetaMask or Trust Wallet.
  3. On the home page of Safestarlink.com click on “Purchase Tokens” button. This will open a Dapp Exchange Called “PancakeSwap”.
  4. A popup will open, click the check box “I understand”, this will load Safestarlink into PancakeSwap.
  5. Make sure your MetaMask Wallet or Trust Wallet Is synced with PancakeSwap.
  6. Set the amount of Safestarlink you would like to purchase , and set slippage to 11%. Then press Swap!
  7. Enjoy your trip to the stars!
